News and Notices

Sanjivani College of Pharmacy is always in the news for its innovative ideas and research

Hon'ble Balasaheb Deoram Wagh Lifetime Achievement Award

Hon'ble Nitindada Kolhe Saheb (Chairman-Sanjivani Group of Institutes) has been honored with the prestigious 'Hon'ble Balasaheb Deoram Wagh Lifetime Achievement Award'

"Best College Award" By Savitribai Phule Pune University

Sanjivani College of Pharmaceutical Education and Research (An Autonomous Institute) has been honoured with the “Best College Award” by Savitribai Phule Pune University on occasion of 75th Amrut Mohastav Year of University.

MSBTE Sponsored Technical Paper Presentation Competition

Ms. Jejurkar Sakshi and Jadhav Samiksha won Second Prize of Rs. 10,000/- in MSBTE Sponsored State Level Technical Paper Presentation Competition held at KCT’s R.G. Sapkal Institute of Pharmacy, Nashik on 6th Feb. 2024.

Celebration of 75th Republic Day on 26th Jan 2024

Sanjivani College of Pharmaceutical Education and Research, Kopargaon had celebrated 75th Republic Day on 26th Jan 2024 in the auspicious Hon. Shri. Nitindada Kolhe Saheb Chairman, Sanjivani Rural Education Society.

State level PIMS (DU) Pharma Cup 2024 Women Volleyball winner

Sanjivani College of Pharmaceutical Education & Research, Ahmednagar, Maharashtra Girls wins State level PIMS (DU) Pharma Cup 2024 Volleyball competition on 22nd Jan 2024 held at PIMS.
